Special moves
-------------


-O + X  (Ice breaker)

-O + A  (Elbow)

Roll = A+X (hold back to roll backward)
Charge power = A+Y
Knockdown attack = Y+B
Throws = at close distance, hold back or forward and press Y or B (Y version can be done in air)
